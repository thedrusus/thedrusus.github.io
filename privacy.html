Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-18

This privacy policy applies to all mobile applications and games published by Fluffy Parrot Studios, together with any related services (collectively, the "Application"). Fluffy Parrot Studios is hereby referred to as the "Service Provider".

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

The Service Provider itself does not collect, log, or store any personally identifying information when you download and use the Application. Registration is not required. Game progress and preferences (such as completed levels, best times, high scores, and audio and control settings) are saved only on your device using your operating system's local storage and are never transmitted to the Service Provider.

If the Application is used with an active internet connection, technical protocol data (such as your ephemeral IP address) is transmitted to facilitate network connectivity. This connectivity is also used by the third-party advertising service described below.

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

This Application does not collect precise GPS location information. The third-party advertising service may infer an approximate (city-level) location from your IP address for the purpose of serving regionally appropriate ads.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

The Application displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob, a service operated by Google LLC. To serve ads, AdMob may collect and process the following information directly from your device:

  • Your device's mobile advertising identifier (the Android Advertising ID, or the iOS Identifier for Advertisers if applicable)
  • IP address (used both for ad delivery and to infer coarse geographic region)
  • Device information such as device model, operating system version, and language
  • Interactions with ads shown in the Application (impressions, clicks)
  • App version and a non-personal session identifier used for ad frequency capping and fraud prevention

This information is collected by Google directly and is not received or stored by the Service Provider. Google uses this data to deliver, measure, and improve advertisements, and to detect invalid or fraudulent ad activity. Google's use of this data is governed by:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services
  • AdMob & AdSense data disclosure

Other than as described in this policy, the Service Provider does not share any data with third parties.

In-app purchases

The Application may offer optional in-app purchases (for example, a one-time purchase to remove advertisements, or other game content). All purchases are processed by the Google Play billing system, operated by Google LLC. The Service Provider does not receive, process, or store your payment-card numbers, billing address, or any other financial information — these are handled solely by Google under the Google Privacy Policy. The Application records only whether an entitlement (such as "ads removed") has been purchased, and this purchase status is stored locally on your device. The Service Provider does not operate user accounts and does not collect personally identifying information through purchases.

What are my opt-out rights?

You can limit ad personalization or reset your advertising identifier at any time through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization"
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, and Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising

Uninstalling the Application stops any further collection by AdMob through the Application.

If you contact the Service Provider directly or voluntarily provide information by other means, you may request deletion of that information by contacting fluffyparrotstudios@gmail.com.

If you voluntarily provide personal information through other means and are under 13 years of age, your parent or guardian must provide consent on your behalf where permitted by law.

Security

However, no security system is completely secure. The Service Provider implements reasonable safeguards to protect systems and any data it holds. Data processed by Google AdMob and Google Play billing is secured by Google in accordance with Google's own privacy and security practices.
